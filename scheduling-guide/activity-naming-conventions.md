## 3. KISS - Keep it simple, Stupid

### Rule# 1: Remember your audience.

The schedule is for the superintendent to use to manage his project. It is not to prove how savy you are with P6, or to justify your hours. If your scheduler takes less than 60 seconds to review the schedule, you‚Äôve lost him. Start over.

1. As a general rule of thumb, there should be no more than 1,000 activities for every $100M in construction volume
2. There should be no more than 100 WBS under any circumstance
3. Keep text concise as possible.
4. Aim for at least 1 WBS on each page
5. If you‚Äôve got more activities taking place in one day then there are workers on site, you‚Äôre doing it wrong
